About NayePankh

Think global, Act local.

NayePankh Foundation is a youth-led, government registered NGO formed with the belief that service begins close to home. What started as a small effort by students during difficult times has grown into a movement for children, families, and communities that need dependable support.

Working across Kanpur, Ghaziabad, and other cities, the foundation focuses on education, food distribution, clothing support, hygiene awareness, women-focused initiatives, and relief during crisis. Its UP Government, 80G, and 12A registrations strengthen the trust behind every campaign and contribution.

2020 Service during crisis

Young volunteers began helping communities affected by the COVID-19 pandemic.

28 Mar 2021 Ground work began

The team formally stepped into field service with youth-led local campaigns.

Today 2 lakh+ people supported

Campaigns now focus on food, clothing, education, hygiene, and relief support.

Purpose

Built around youth leadership, dignity, and action on the ground.

M

Mission

To uplift underprivileged communities by connecting people with education, food, clothing, hygiene support, and timely relief.

V

Vision

To give wings to those held back by poverty and create a society where every child and family can live with opportunity and dignity.

C

Founder's Belief

Inspired by Prashant Shukla's message that shared effort can solve real problems, the foundation turns individual support into collective change.

Programs

Practical initiatives shaped by what communities need most.

01

Education Support

Classes, mentoring, study material support, and encouragement for children who deserve a fair chance to continue learning.

02

Health Awareness

Awareness drives on personal hygiene, menstrual health, sanitation, and preventive care for women, youth, and families.

03

Food Distribution

Meal and ration distribution for people facing hunger, especially during difficult seasons, emergencies, and community-level need.

04

Women Empowerment

Women-focused campaigns that promote dignity, menstrual hygiene awareness, confidence, and access to essential support.

05

Crisis Relief

Volunteer-led support during crisis situations through essentials, field coordination, and quick response for vulnerable families.

06

Community Development

Local campaigns for clothing support, sanitation awareness, youth participation, and stronger neighborhood-level care networks.
