Why Donate

Help break the cycle of poverty and hopelessness.

NayePankh Foundation was founded with a simple belief: every child deserves the right to dream, learn, and grow with dignity, regardless of socio-economic background.

Many children and families still struggle without proper education, healthcare, sanitation, clean water, food, and basic necessities. Your support helps the foundation continue reaching communities that need practical care and long-term hope.

Prashant Shukla Founder & President, NayePankh Foundation
